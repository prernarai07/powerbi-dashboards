# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard — Power BI

![Mobile Sales Dashboard](./dashboard-preview.png)

An interactive Power BI dashboard analysing mobile phone sales performance across Indian cities, brands, payment methods, and time periods — with dynamic slicers for real-time filtering.

---

## Dashboard Overview

**File:** `Mobile_Sales_Dashboard.pbix`  
**Data Source:** Mobile sales transaction data (CSV)  
**Tool:** Power BI Desktop 2026

---

## Key Metrics (KPI Cards)

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Sales | 68M |
| Total Transactions | 2K |
| Total Quantity | 331 |
| Avg. Sales per Transaction | 41K |

---

## Visuals & Charts

| Visual | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Total Sales by City | Donut Chart | City-wise sales distribution across 15+ Indian cities |
| Total Quantity by Month | Line Chart | Monthly volume trend throughout the year |
| Customer Ratings | Bar Chart | Distribution of ratings (1–5 stars) |
| Transactions by Payment Method | Pie Chart | Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Cash split |
| Total Sales by Mobile Model | Bar Chart | Top performing models (iPhone SE, OnePlus, Galaxy S21) |
| Total Sales by Day Name | Area Chart | Day-of-week sales pattern |
| Brand Summary Table | Table | Xiaomi, Samsung, Vivo, OnePlus, Apple — sales, quantity, transactions |
| Month Slicer | Navigation | January–December month filter |
| Slicers | Filters | Mobile Model, Payment Method, Brand, Day Name |

---

## Key Insights

- **Delhi** leads city-wise sales with 26.87% share
- **Apple** has the highest total sales among brands at 15.38M
- **iPhone SE** is the top-selling model at 6.6M
- Payment methods are nearly evenly split across Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, and Cash
- **Monday** shows the highest day-of-week sales at 12.0M
